Welcome to Amende & Schultz

Welcome to Amende and Schultz, where passion for seafood excellence has been our driving force for almost Seven decades. As the proud stewards of a legacy that spans generations, we take great pride in offering you the finest selection of Wild caught frozen Shrimp and Crab.

Our Legacy

For almost 70 years, we have remained dedicated to providing you with an unparalleled seafood experience. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to deliver exceptional quality and flavor to your table, while upholding the highest standards of quality.

Our Commitment

At Amende and Schultz, we believe in fostering lasting relationships—with our customers, our suppliers, and the ocean itself. Through our unwavering commitment to quality assurance and environmental stewardship, we ensure that every product that bears our name meets the highest standards of excellence.

Freshness Guaranteed

Our shrimp and king crab are meticulously selected from the bountiful waters of Mexico, where rich marine biodiversity and pristine fishing grounds yield some of the finest seafood in the world. By partnering with local fishermen and suppliers who share our values, we guarantee that each bite you take is a testament to our dedication to freshness, flavor, and sustainability. As we look to the future, our commitment to you remains stronger than ever.

Innovation and Excellence

We are continually exploring new ways to innovate and elevate your seafood experience, while remaining true to the timeless principles that have defined us for over half a century.
